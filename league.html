Halo 4

Structure/Scoring

  • Every player will play at least one match per night, with second matches and "make up" matches being played afterword if time allows.
  • Each player's total Score from the matches will be recorded.
  • The average of each player's best 6 scores will be used as his or her League Rank Score.
  • During the League, players will be assigned randomly to a teammate from the league for 2v2 matches.
  • League Scoring will be based off of match performance scores.
  • We will take the top 6(six) performances over the course of the League that will determine your placement in the final tournament.
